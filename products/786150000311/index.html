Description:

Leffe Blonde is a traditional Belgian abbey beer with origins dating back to 1240. Known for its smooth, well-balanced character, it features subtle notes of vanilla, clove, and ripe fruit, offering a refined and elegant taste.


Ingredients:

Water, barley malt, maize, hops, yeast.


Consumption precautions:

Contains alcohol. Not recommended for children, pregnant or breastfeeding women. Please consume responsibly.


History

1240
Leffe originated in the Notre-Dame de Leffe Abbey in Belgium, where monks brewed beer as part of daily life.
1952
The Leffe beer tradition was revived, preserving centuries-old abbey brewing heritage.
