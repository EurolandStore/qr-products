Description

Leffe Blonde is a distinguished Belgian abbey beer with origins dating back to 1240. Brewed in the heart of Belgium, it is crafted with rich malts and noble hops, offering a smooth and balanced profile with delicate notes of vanilla, clove, and ripe fruit. Its refined character and gentle finish make it a timeless classic enjoyed around the world.

Ingredients

Water, barley malt, maize, hops, yeast.

Consumption precautions

Contains alcohol. Not recommended for children, pregnant or breastfeeding women. Please consume responsibly.

History
1240
Leffe originated at the Notre-Dame de Leffe Abbey in Belgium, where monks brewed beer as part of daily monastic life.
1952
The Leffe beer tradition was revived, preserving centuries-old abbey brewing heritage and bringing it to a global audience.
